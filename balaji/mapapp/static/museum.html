Ooty

Government Museum

  • Established in 1989, the museum is managed by the Tamil Nadu Department of Museums.
  • The museum building reflects British colonial architecture with a blend of modern elements.
  • The museum showcases the rich cultural heritage, natural history, and art of the Nilgiri region.
  • Geology Section: Displays rocks, minerals, and fossils from the region. Botany Section: Features a variety of preserved plant species native to the Nilgiris. Zoology Section: Houses a collection of animal specimens, including butterflies, birds, and insects. Anthropology Section: Displays tribal artifacts and cultural items related to the indigenous people of the Nilgiris.
